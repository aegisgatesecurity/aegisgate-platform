## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

### 2.1 "Confidential Information"
Means any information disclosed by one Party to the other, whether orally, in writing, or by inspection, that is designated as "Confidential," "Proprietary," or with a similar legend, or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

### 2.2 Examples
Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:
- Business plans, strategies, and forecasts
- Customer lists and prospect information
- Technical information, including software, algorithms, and architecture
- Pricing and financial information
- Trade secrets and know-how
- Product roadmaps and feature specifications

### 2.3 Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that:
- (a) Was publicly known at the time of disclosure
- (b) Becomes publicly known through no fault of the Recipient
- (c) Was known to Recipient prior to disclosure (with documented evidence)
- (d) Is independently developed by Recipient without use of Confidential Information
- (e) Is lawfully obtained from a third party without restriction

## 3. OBLIGATIONS

### 3.1 Use of Confidential Information
Recipient shall:
- (a) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
- (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent
- (c) Protect Confidential Information using reasonable care (no less than how Recipient protects its own confidential information)
- (d) Limit access to employees and contractors with a need to know

### 3.2 Permitted Disclosures
Recipient may disclose Confidential Information to the extent required by law, provided that Recipient:
- (a) Gives the disclosing Party prompt written notice
- (b) Cooperates with efforts to obtain protective orders
- (c) Discloses only the minimum necessary

### 3.3 Return of Information
Upon request or termination, Recipient shall promptly:
- (a) Return or destroy all Confidential Information
- (b) Certify in writing that such return or destruction is complete

### 3.4 No License
Nothing in this Agreement grants Recipient any license or right to any intellectual property.
