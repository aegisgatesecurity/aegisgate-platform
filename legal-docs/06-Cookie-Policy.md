## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners useful information.

### Types of Cookies We Use

| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Essential Cookies** | Required for the website to function properly. They enable core features like security, network management, and accessibility. | Session / Persistent |
| **Analytics Cookies** | Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. | Persistent |
| **Functional Cookies** | Enable enhanced features like remembering your preferences and settings. | Persistent |
| **Marketing Cookies** | Used to track visitors across websites for advertising purposes. | Persistent |

## 2. HOW WE USE COOKIES

### 2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for our website to function. Without them, certain features would not work.

**Examples:**
- Remembering your cookie consent preferences
- Maintaining secure sessions
- Load balancing and performance optimization

**Duration:** Session or up to 1 year

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how you and other visitors use our website.

**What we collect:**
- Pages visited and time spent on pages
- Navigation patterns
- Device and browser information
- Referral sources

**Tools we use:**
- Google Analytics (anonymized)
- Our own internal analytics

**Duration:** Up to 2 years

### 2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

**Examples:**
- Remembering your language preference
- Storing your account settings
- Remembering recently viewed documentation

**Duration:** Up to 1 year

### 2.4 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:
- **Accept All** — Accept all cookie categories
- **Decline** — Decline non-essential cookies
- **Customize** — Choose which cookie categories to accept
- **Learn More** — View this Cookie Policy

Your preferences will be stored for 12 months.

## 5. YOUR CHOICES REGARDING COOKIES

### 5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies through our cookie consent banner. Your preferences will be remembered for 12 months.

### 5.2 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and site data
2. Choose "Block third-party cookies" or customize

**Firefox:**
1. Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Customize your settings

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
2. Manage cookie settings

**Edge:**
1. Settings → Privacy and services → Cookies
2. Customize your settings

### 5.3 Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of interest-based advertising through your device settings.

### 5.4 Global Privacy Control

We respect the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser or device is configured with GPC, we will treat this as a valid opt-out request for the sale/sharing of personal information.
