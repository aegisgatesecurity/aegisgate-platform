## 2. FUTURE SUBPROCESSORS

### 2.1 Notification Process
Before engaging a new Subprocessor, we will:
1. Evaluate the Subprocessor's security and privacy practices
2. Enter into a data processing agreement with the Subprocessor
3. Notify customers via email at least 30 days before adding a new Subprocessor

### 2.2 Objection Right
Customers may object to the use of a new Subprocessor by contacting us within 30 days of notification. We will work with the customer to address concerns.

---

## 3. SUBPROCESSOR SAFEGUARDS

### 3.1 Contractual Requirements
All Subprocessors are required to:
- Process personal data only on documented instructions
- Maintain appropriate security measures
- Comply with applicable data protection laws
- Allow audits and inspections

### 3.2 Liability
We remain liable to our customers for the performance of our Subprocessors' obligations.
